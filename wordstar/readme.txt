== Description ==
This theme is clean, blog-focused, and designed for clarity, with simple, straightforward typography that's readable on a wide variety of screen sizes, and suitable for multiple languages.This theme, like WordPress, is licensed under the GPL. Use it to make something cool, have fun, and share what you've learned with others.

* User can add their social media profile links(Twitter, Facebook,Instagram, Google Plus, Flickr, Github,..)
* Social Widget Area (Create a custom Menu with your social profile links and add in Social Widget Area as widget to appear in your sidebar.)
* Responsive Layout
* Custom Logo
* Custom Colors
* Custom Header
* Post Formats
* Semantics (microdata/schema.org)
* RTL
* The GPL v3.0 or later license. :) 

Use it to make something cool.


== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in "WordStar" in the search form and press the 'Enter' key in your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
5. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel.
